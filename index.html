About Us

The Plateau Publishing Co. is your community-rooted source for connecting the people and stories of Enumclaw, Bonney Lake, and the greater Plateau. We’re proudly independent, not franchised — a truly local publication sharing stories that matter to our neighbors.

Our Magazines

The Plateau: Enumclaw Edition and The Plateau: Bonney Lake Edition are monthly magazines created for everyone who calls the Plateau home. From community stories and local business spotlights to events, art, and outdoor life — we capture what makes Enumclaw shine.

Marketing & Sponsorship

Reach your neighbors through a blend of targeted print + digital marketing. The Plateau Publishing Co. offers opportunities for authentic brand connection, content sponsorship, and featured local storytelling — tailored for the small-town feel Enumclaw loves.
